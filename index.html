About Us

Opoint Consulting MMC is a link between job seekers and employment agencies across Europe. We exist to make international employment simpler, safer, and more effective — for both sides.

Many individuals, especially from Azerbaijan and neighboring regions, face difficulties finding real job opportunities abroad. At the same time, many employment agencies in Europe struggle to find qualified and reliable workers. We bring these two needs together through a structured and transparent system.

Our company operates as a professional network where job seekers and employment agencies connect — guided by clear processes, legal standards, and mutual understanding. We do not sell job vacancies or demand high fees. Instead, we focus on delivering real value and honest support to both parties.

What We Do

Opoint Consulting offers two core services — one for job seekers and one for employment agencies:

Guide – For Job Seekers
We help candidates navigate the entire employment journey abroad — from preparation to arrival. Our services include: explaining job roles and contracts, preparing documents, supporting visa applications, and connecting candidates with verified agencies.

Recruitment – For Employment Agencies
We assist European employment agencies in strengthening their workforce with prepared, pre-qualified candidates. We match workers to specific job needs, verify documentation, and ensure both sides are fully informed before the process begins.

We operate in full accordance with international employment laws and the official requirements set by embassies for visa and work permit processing. While we have no affiliation with any embassy or government institution, our role is to ensure that all documentation is complete, accurate, and compliant — giving both the worker and the employer the best possible chance of a smooth, legal recruitment outcome.
